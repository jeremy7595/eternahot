Service plans cover Rinnai tankless water heaters only. We service every make and model, but the monthly plan is for Rinnai tankless systems.

Every unit, identified

Each enrolled unit is identified by its serial number and documented at your initial inspection. Coverage is tied to those exact units — enroll every system you'd like covered.

Monthly billing

Commercial Duty from $30/unit and Severe Service from $55/unit, with progressive multi-unit discounts applied automatically. Initiation fee charged at checkout, monthly payments begin in 30 days. Month-to-month — cancel anytime.

What's covered

Your monthly payment covers scheduled maintenance (flush, descale & inspection), priority scheduling, direct phone access to our techs, and member rates on all service — and it keeps your manufacturer warranty valid.

A $50 fuel surcharge applies any time we dispatch a technician, to cover California fuel costs. Scheduled preventive maintenance has no surcharge.

FAQ

Common questions

Which water heaters do the plans cover?
Rinnai tankless water heaters. We service every make and model on the repair side, but the monthly service plans are for Rinnai tankless systems only.
What's the difference between Commercial Duty and Severe Service?
Commercial Duty ($30/unit/mo base) is for typical systems with annual maintenance. Severe Service ($55/unit/mo base) is for units operating under severe conditions — running at 145°F or higher, water hardness above 200 mg/L or demonstrated dissolved-solids issues, or on track to average more than 3,000 hours a year — and includes more intensive maintenance to match the heavier wear. Both plans carry the same progressive multi-unit discounts.
How do the multi-unit discounts work?
They're progressive and automatic. Each additional unit's monthly rate is 10% lower than the previous unit's, down to a maximum discount of 50% per unit. The same applies to the one-time initiation fee: $300 for the first unit, each additional unit 10% less, down to $150. The signup form above shows your exact price as you change the unit count.
Can I negotiate the price?
No — and that's by design. Prices and discounts are set by the program and applied automatically at checkout. Our employees and technicians cannot modify, discount, or negotiate them for anyone, so every customer with the same number of enrolled units pays exactly the same price. Want a bigger discount? Enroll more units.
What am I charged at signup?
Just the one-time initiation fee for your units — $300 for the first, discounted for each additional one. That covers your first maintenance visit and inspection, where we document each unit's condition and record its serial number. Your card is saved at signup, and monthly payments begin in 30 days.
How does the 30-day free trial work?
Your plan benefits start immediately, but the monthly fee doesn't. At checkout you pay only the initiation fee; your card is saved, and the first monthly charge happens on day 31. Cancel within the first 30 days and you owe no monthly fees.
Is the plan for homes or businesses?
Both. Choose the duty level that matches how hard your systems work, enroll every Rinnai tankless unit at the property, and the multi-unit discounts apply automatically either way.
What's covered, and what's billed separately?
Your monthly payment covers scheduled maintenance (flush, descale & inspection), priority scheduling, phone access to our techs, member rates on all service, and no overtime charges. Repairs (parts & labor) are quoted at member rates and approved before we proceed; equipment replacements, pipe repairs, and permits are billed separately.
Do members pay overtime for nights or weekends?
No. As a member you pay the same rate any hour — nights, weekends, and holidays included — with no after-hours or overtime premium.
How do diagnostics and repairs work?
We come diagnose the problem, then quote any repair at member rates for your approval — nothing gets fixed without your okay. If you have us do the repair, your diagnostic fee is credited toward it.
What is the $50 fuel surcharge?
It applies per service call — any time you ask us to dispatch a technician — to cover fuel costs in California. Regularly scheduled maintenance visits have no surcharge.
What happens if I miss payments?
Your plan is active service you use — not a balance you build up. If a payment fails we'll email you to update your card and retry it automatically; if it stays unpaid the plan cancels and your benefits stop. Your discounts remain in effect as long as your plan is active and payments are current — but if unpaid invoices cause cancellation, re-enrollment requires the full, undiscounted initiation fee for every unit, because lapsed units must be re-inspected and re-onboarded.
Can I cancel?
Yes — the plan is month-to-month. Cancel anytime, including during your free 30 days.
