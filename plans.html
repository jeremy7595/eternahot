The fine print

Clear terms, no surprises

Monthly billing

Plans bill automatically each month and continue until cancelled. Maintenance is month-to-month; Business-Crucial carries a 12-month minimum term.

Fuel surcharge

A $50 fuel surcharge applies any time you request a technician on-site (emergency or on-demand visits). Regularly scheduled maintenance visits have no surcharge.

Permits

Any required permits are billed separately and are the customer's responsibility.

Initiation fee

A one-time $300 initiation applies at signup and includes your initial service and inspection.

Missed payments

More than two missed payments ends membership. Re-entry requires a brand-new plan (new initiation and initial service) — no back-paying to catch up.

Full service agreement

The complete terms — eligibility & installation standards, equipment ownership, performance standards, and service conditions — are detailed in the Eternahot service agreement you'll receive at enrollment.

FAQ

Common questions

Is the Maintenance Plan for homes or businesses?
Both — it's $30/month per unit either way, and includes an annual flush & maintenance, phone access to our techs, and one free service call per year (up to 2 hours of labor).
What is the fuel surcharge?
$50, any time you ask us to dispatch a technician (emergency or on-demand visits, or moving the truck for parking). Regularly scheduled maintenance has no surcharge.
How does Business-Crucial guarantee continuity?
We purchase and stage identical standby units (our property) at your site. If a covered unit fails, we swap it immediately so your hot water stays online. It covers new Rinnai CX systems with sizing and piping we've approved.
Can I cancel?
The Maintenance Plan is month-to-month. Business-Crucial carries a 12-month minimum term because we stage dedicated standby equipment for your site.
