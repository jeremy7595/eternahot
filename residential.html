Common Questions

Everything You Want to Know.

How long does a residential install take?

Most residential installations are completed in 4–6 hours. If significant gas line work or venting modifications are needed, it may run a full day. We'll give you a realistic timeline at the time of assessment.

Do I need a permit?

Yes — Los Angeles requires a permit for water heater replacements and new installations. We handle the permit application as part of every installation. Don't let any contractor skip this — it protects you when you sell your home.

How much will I save on my gas bill?

Most homeowners see a 25–40% reduction in water heating costs. For an average LA family, that's typically $200–$400 per year in savings — often offsetting the cost of the unit over time.

How often should I have my unit serviced?

Annually in Southern California — our hard water builds mineral scale inside the heat exchanger quickly. We'll send you a reminder. An annual descale takes 1–2 hours and extends your unit's life by years.

Do you offer financing?

Yes — financing is available for qualifying installations. Monthly payments often work out to less than what you save on your gas bill, making the upgrade essentially self-funding. Ask us for current terms when you get your quote.
