FAQ

Questions we hear a lot

Do you only work on tankless water heaters?
Tankless is our specialty, but no — we install, maintain, and repair every system that produces potable hot water: traditional tanks, hybrid heat-pump units, high-efficiency models, and DHW off commercial boilers.
Do you handle commercial projects?
Yes — commercial is the majority of our work. We size and install systems for restaurants, multi-family, hospitality, medical, and more, and offer planned maintenance with priority service response.
Is tankless worth it versus a standard tank?
For most of our clients, yes: endless hot water, higher efficiency, a smaller footprint, and a longer service life when maintained. We'll give you an honest comparison for your specific demand and budget rather than a one-size-fits-all answer.
How often should a tankless unit be serviced?
Typically once a year — more often in hard-water areas or high-demand commercial settings. Regular descaling and flushing protect performance and keep the manufacturer warranty valid. Ask us about a service agreement.
My commercial system is down — can you respond fast?
A hot-water outage is an emergency, especially for a business. Call us at (213) 222-3457 and we'll get you scheduled and back online as quickly as possible.
