FREQUENTLY ASKED

Questions we hear every week.

How fast can you get here for no hot water? ⌄
We offer true 24/7 emergency response. Most Los Angeles and nearby calls are answered the same day. Call 213.222.3457 and we’ll give you an honest ETA immediately.
Are you an actual authorized Rinnai installer? ⌄
Yes. We are an authorized Rinnai specialist (not just “we install Rinnai”). This means proper training, access to technical support, and full warranty support on the equipment we install and service.
Do tankless water heaters really save money? ⌄
Yes — typically 25–40% on water heating costs in Southern California homes because you only heat water when you need it. You also get unlimited hot water and the units last longer (often 15–20+ years with proper maintenance).
Is the $199 Health Check worth it? ⌄
Almost always. Hard water in LA and surrounding areas builds scale fast. A proper annual flush + inspection catches small issues before they become expensive failures and keeps your efficiency high.
What areas do you serve? ⌄
All of Los Angeles, Santa Monica, Beverly Hills, Pasadena, Manhattan Beach, West Hollywood, Culver City, and greater Orange County. If you’re in Southern California and have a tankless or hot water system, we can almost certainly help.
Do you work on other brands besides Rinnai? ⌄
Yes. We service and install Navien, Rheem, Takagi, Noritz, Bosch, and most other quality tankless brands. Our deep Rinnai expertise gives us an advantage on diagnostics across the category.
