Industries We Serve

We Know Your Hot Water Demands.

Every type of commercial facility has different peak demand patterns. We've engineered solutions for them all across Southern California.

🍽️ Restaurants & Food Service

High-volume dishwashing, prep sinks, and hand-wash stations run all day. We design systems that keep up during peak service hours without a single cold moment. Health code compliance built in.

🏨 Hotels & Hospitality

Guest satisfaction lives and dies on hot water. We design recirculating systems so every room — on every floor — has instant hot water, all day, no matter occupancy.

🏢 Multifamily & Apartment Buildings

Multiple units, multiple morning peaks. We engineer central systems and recirculation loops sized for your specific unit count and plumbing layout, so every tenant stays satisfied simultaneously.

🚿 Car Washes

Spot-free rinse systems and heated wash bays need consistent, high-volume hot water. We engineer tankless systems that handle continuous throughput without the lag of tank storage.

🧖 Spas & Wellness Centers

Hydrotherapy pools, steam rooms, showers, and treatment rooms demand precise, reliable water temperatures at all hours. We design systems that meet those standards without compromise.

🎰 Casinos & Entertainment Venues

24/7 operations with restaurants, hotels, and spa facilities all under one roof. We handle the complexity of multi-zone systems with redundancy built in — because downtime isn't an option.

🏥 Elderly Care & Assisted Living

Bathing schedules, laundry demands, and scald-prevention requirements make senior living facilities a specialized install. We understand the codes and engineer systems that meet them.

🏋️ Gyms & Fitness Centers

Post-workout shower rushes hit hard and fast. Tankless handles simultaneous demand that would drain a conventional tank system within minutes. No cold showers, no complaints.

🏭 Industrial & Manufacturing

Process hot water, wash stations, facility restrooms — we size systems for continuous industrial demand with service contracts that keep you running and compliant year-round.
