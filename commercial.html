Industries We Serve

We know your hot water demands.

Every facility has its own peak-demand pattern. We’ve engineered solutions for them all across Southern California.

Restaurants & Food Service

High-volume dishwashing, prep sinks, and hand-wash stations run all day. We design systems that keep up through peak service without a single cold moment — health-code compliance built in.

Hotels & Hospitality

Guest satisfaction lives and dies on hot water. We design recirculating systems so every room, on every floor, has instant hot water at any occupancy.

Multifamily & HOAs

Multiple units, multiple morning peaks. We engineer central systems and recirculation loops sized to your unit count and plumbing layout, so every tenant is satisfied simultaneously.

Car Washes

Spot-free rinse systems and heated bays need consistent, high-volume hot water. We engineer tankless systems that handle continuous throughput without the lag of tank storage.

Spas & Wellness

Hydrotherapy pools, steam rooms, and treatment rooms demand precise, reliable temperatures at all hours. We design systems that meet those standards without compromise.

Casinos & Entertainment

Around-the-clock operations with restaurants, hotels, and spas under one roof. We handle multi-zone systems with redundancy built in — because downtime isn’t an option.

Elderly Care & Assisted Living

Bathing schedules, laundry demands, and scald-prevention requirements make senior facilities a specialized install. We understand the codes and engineer systems that meet them.

Gyms & Fitness

Post-workout shower rushes hit hard and fast. Tankless handles simultaneous demand that would drain a conventional tank within minutes — no cold showers, no complaints.

Industrial & Manufacturing

Process hot water, wash stations, facility restrooms — we size systems for continuous industrial demand, with service contracts that keep you running and compliant year-round.
