# "What NOT to do" — photo drop

Real field photos for the future `/what-not-to-do` gallery go here. (See the
full idea in `docs/ideas.md`.)

## How to collect (on the go)

1. Snap photos of bad piping / configurations **and their consequences** in the
   field.
2. Save them to your **Google Drive → Eternahot** folder — works from the
   Chromebook while you're out of town. Suggested subfolder:
   `Eternahot/what-not-to-do`.
3. When you have a batch, the finalists get copied into **this folder** in the
   repo, and we build the page from them.

## Naming (makes the page easy to build)

Name each file so the problem — and ideally the consequence — is obvious. Use
`--` to separate the problem from the consequence:

- `gas-flex-instead-of-hardpipe.jpg`
- `no-isolation-valves--scale-buildup.jpg`
- `recirc-piped-backwards--short-cycling.jpg`
- `undersized-gas-line--unit-faulting.jpg`

## Notes

- Real photos only — no stock.
- Don't include anything that identifies another company or person.
- JPG or PNG is fine; phone photos are perfect. No need to resize — we'll
  optimize them when the page is built.
